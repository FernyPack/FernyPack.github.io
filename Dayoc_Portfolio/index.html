About

I showcase my skills by creating webpages through various projects and assignments. One of the key highlights is the games I’ve made. When I first started, I had no idea where to even begin, and the learning curve seemed steep. However, through a mix of guidance, tutorials, and a lot of self-learning, I was able to figure out how to develop a game from scratch. This hands-on experience has taught me not only how to work with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript but also how to approach problem-solving in coding. Earning my certificate has been a big confidence booster, and it made me realize how much progress I've made in my program. It showed me that there’s still a lot more to learn and room to improve, which motivates me to keep going. My portfolio is a reflection of this growth, showcasing my knowledge and skills with an intuitive design, smooth navigation, and animations that make the user experience engaging. It’s more than just a collection of projects; it’s a way for me to demonstrate how far I’ve come and the direction I’m heading in my web development journey.
